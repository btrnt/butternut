Butternut Analytics

Using Giant Language model Test Room (GLTR), your text was analyzed word by word based on its immediate left word. GLTR assigns a rank and colour based on how predictable the word is.

Colour Guide

More predictable tokens as indicated by the highlight are more likely to be AI-generated while less predictable (redder) tokens are more likely to be human-made. AI-generated text tends to have largely predictable (greener) text while human-written text tends to have more variety and unpredictability
