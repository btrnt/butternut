Butternut Analytics

Using Giant Language model Test Room (GLTR), your text was analyzed word by word based on its immediate left word. GLTR assigns a rank and colour based on how predictable the word is.

Colour Guide

The guide serves as a reference to show how likely or unlikely the highlighted word was generated by AI based on its ranking of predictability.
